African Delights

  • Appetizers

    • Samosas

      Crispy pastry pockets filled with a savory mixture of spiced potatoes, peas, and onions. These golden-brown treats are served with a tangy tamarind chutney.


    • Plantain Chips

      Thinly sliced plantains, fried until crispy and lightly salted. Served with a spicy pepper sauce for an extra kick.


    • Nigerian Pepper Soup

      A spicy and aromatic broth made with a blend of traditional African spices, tender pieces of meat, and fresh herbs. Perfectly warming and flavorful.

  • Main Courses

    • Jollof Rice

      A beloved West African dish of rice cooked in a rich, tomato-based sauce with a blend of aromatic spices. Often accompanied by grilled chicken or beef.


    • Bunny Chow

      A South African favorite consisting of a hollowed-out loaf of bread filled with a hearty curry of your choice, including options like chicken, lamb, or vegetable.


    • Egusi Soup

      A thick and flavorful soup made from ground melon seeds, cooked with vegetables, meat, and fish. Served with pounded yam or fufu for a traditional Nigerian meal.

  • Desserts

    • Mandazi

      Sweet, fried dough treats with a light, fluffy texture. Often enjoyed with a dusting of powdered sugar or served with a side of honey.


    • Chin Chin

      Crunchy, deep-fried dough bites that are slightly sweet and addictive. Perfectly paired with a hot cup of African tea.


    • Malva Pudding

      A South African dessert that is a soft, spongy pudding with a caramelized sauce. Served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.
